Loan Origination System (LOS)

A loan origination system on Salesforce manages the loan process from start to finish, collecting applications and storing documents. It checks credit scores, approves loans, and ensures legal compliance. It manages borrower interactions, releases loan funds, and tracks performance.

Sample Management Solution (SMS)

A sample management solution in Salesforce tracks and manages product samples or promotional items throughout their lifecycle. It includes features for real-time inventory management, efficient order processing, insightful reporting, and seamless integration with other systems, tailored to enhance operational efficiency and visibility.

Clinic Management Solution (CMS)

A clinic management solution on Salesforce simplifies patient management, appointments, billing, and health records. It boosts efficiency, improves care coordination, and offers valuable insights for better decision-making in healthcare settings.


Dealership Management Solution (DMS)

A dealer management solution on Salesforce simplifies dealer operations, sales tracking, and customer relationships. It integrates inventory management, lead handling, and customer support within Salesforce CRM, boosting efficiency and enhancing customer satisfaction with real-time insights for better decision-making.
